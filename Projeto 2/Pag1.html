Andromeda Galaxy

Description

Andromeda Galaxy's (Messier 31, NGC 224) it's a spiral galaxy located about 2,54 million light-years away from earth

interesting data

  1. Besides the Milky Way, it is the most studied galaxy
  2. it's two satellite galaxies, messier 32 and Messier 110, are visible with binoculars
  3. It's distance from earth is still not well defined
